Coffee Shop Revenue Analysis
A full end-to-end data analysis project examining 3,547 point-of-sale transactions from a coffee shop across March 2024 to March 2025. The goal was to identify revenue patterns across products, days, and months and translate findings into actionable business recommendations.
Key Findings

Americano with Milk is the most popular product but underpriced relative to its sales volume, resulting in lost revenue on every transaction
Weekend revenue is significantly lower than weekday performance, indicating an over-reliance on a commuter customer base
January and April are consistently the weakest months, tied to reduced commuter activity during holiday periods
Peak trading hours are 10am and 4pm with stable performance between them

Recommendations
Three data-backed recommendations were produced: repricing underperforming products, partnering with local community groups to drive weekend footfall, and reducing stock and staffing costs during predictable slow months.
